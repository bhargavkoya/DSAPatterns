Reverse Linked List
📌
Reverse the links in a singly linked list (iterative or recursive).
🎯
Why it matters:
Appears in almost every LinkedIn, Meta, Amazon round as a base concept.
Core to many composite problems (e.g., palindrome checks, group reversals).
🔁
Variants:
Full reverse
Reverse sublist (between m and n)
Reverse every k nodes
🔥
Examples:
